B.Tech Civil Engineering

DURATION : 4 YRS. (8 SEMESTERS)

The Department of Civil Engineering at University Teaching Department, CSVTU, Bhilai offers comprehensive undergraduate (B.Tech.) and postgraduate (M.Tech.) programs. Our curriculum, aligned with the Chhattisgarh Swami Vivekananda Technical University (CSVTU), Bhilai syllabus, covers critical aspects of civil engineering, including the planning, design, construction, and management of infrastructure projects. Our well-qualified and experienced faculty members deliver high-quality education and foster a supportive learning environment. Their expertise spans various specializations, providing students with a broad and in-depth understanding of the field. Civil engineering is a profession dedicated to improving quality of life by creating and maintaining essential infrastructure. Our curriculum emphasizes both practical and theoretical knowledge, preparing students to design sustainable buildings, transportation systems, water resource systems and environmental solutions. The department's state-of-the-art laboratories offer hands-on experience in civil engineering practices, allowing students to apply classroom knowledge to real-world scenarios and fostering innovation and technical proficiency.

Vision

  • To be a premier hub of civil engineering education and research, our department aims to cultivate visionary engineers equipped with cutting-edge skills and ethical values. We strive to lead in fostering sustainable infrastructure solutions that positively impact society.

Mission

  • Equip students with comprehensive engineering knowledge and diverse skill sets grounded in ethical principles.
  • Foster a culture of curiosity and encourage innovative, creative, and ethical thinking.
  • Develop competent professionals and entrepreneurs who embrace responsibility and contribute to societal betterment.
  • Create an academic environment that supports continuous improvement and prepares students for evolving challenges in civil engineering.

PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES (PEOs)

  • ✔ Cultivate civil engineering graduates through a dynamic and adaptable Outcome-Based curriculum, integrating technical, societal, ethical, and environmental considerations.
  • ✔ Equip graduates with proficiency in applying state-of-the-art technology through continuous enhancement of infrastructure, pedagogical tools, and human resources.
  • ✔ Develop skilled civil engineering professionals with a passion for lifelong learning and innovation in Research and Development.
  • ✔ Foster civil engineering professionals with a balanced blend of robust technical knowledge, professional values, and ethics, preparing them for employment, entrepreneurship, higher education, and societal service.

PROGRAMME OUTCOMES

  • Engineering knowledge: Apply the knowledge of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals, and an engineering specialization to the solution of complex engineering problems.
  • Problem analysis: Identify, formulate, research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions using first principles of mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering sciences to arrive at substantiated conclusions.
  • Design/Development of solutions: Design solutions for complex civil engineering problems, considering public health, safety, cultural, societal, and environmental factors.
  • Complex Problem Investigations: Utilize research-based knowledge and methods, including experimental design, data analysis, and synthesis, to provide valid conclusions.
  • Modern Tool Usage: Employ appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering and IT tools, including prediction and modelling, understanding their limitations in complex engineering activities.
  • Engineering and Society: Apply contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal, and cultural issues in professional engineering practice.
  • Environment and Sustainability: Understand the impact of engineering solutions in societal and environmental contexts, demonstrating knowledge of sustainable development principles.
  • Ethics: Apply ethical principles and commit to professional ethics and responsibilities in engineering practice.
  • Individual and team work: Function effectively in diverse teams, including leadership roles, and in multidisciplinary settings.
  • Communication: Communicate effectively with the engineering community and society through reports, design documentation, presentations, and instructions.
  • Project Management and Finance: Apply engineering and management principles to manage projects individually and in teams, including in multidisciplinary environments.
  • Lifelong Learning: Recognize the need for and engage in independent and lifelong learning in response to technological advancements.

Programme Specific Outcome (PSOs)

  • Provide safe, economical, and practical solutions to diverse civil engineering problems.
  • Function effectively as a member or leader of technical teams and manage civil engineering projects, including those of multidisciplinary nature.
  • Visualize and propose innovative solutions to challenges where existing approaches may be inadequate.
  • Demonstrate a commitment to professional ethics, emphasizing quality, timeliness, and continuous improvement in engineering practice.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Duration: 4 yrs. The Candidates should have passed Senior Secondary (10+2) or an equivalent Examination in five subjects taken together including compulsory subjects of Physics, Mathematics and one subject of Chemistry/ Biotechnology/ Biology/ Computer Science & English with 60% marks in aggregate (40% marks in the case of SC/ ST Category). Or Passed Diploma examination with at least 60% marks (40% in case of candidates belonging to reserved category), subject to vacancies in the First Year, in case the vacancies at lateral entry are exhausted.
