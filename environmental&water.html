M.Tech Environmental & Water Resource Engineering

DURATION : 2 YRS. (4 SEMESTERS)

The department was established on 2017 in University Teaching Department. The department offers application based Postgraduate (M.Tech) Degree program in water resources development, irrigation, climate change impacts, remote sensing and GIS in water resources and environment sustainability. The department is vigorously involved in research and development in the area of water resources engineering and management. Also, department provides consultancy services for flood & drought control, water supply (irrigation, domestic, etc.), groundwater development and water resources sustainability. The main objective of the department is to develop manpower that can take a part of sustainable development and management of available water resources.

University is commencing M.Tech from 2020-21. The nomenclature of the degree are as follows:

  • M.Tech Environmental & Water Resource Engineering – 18 Seats

Facilities available

  • The department is well equipped with high end workstations, server systems and various software for image processing, GeoSpatial data handling and land use land cover classification (ERDAS Imagine, GeoMedia, TerrSet and ArcGIS), software for rainfall-runoff modelling including flood inundation mapping (MIKE Hydro Basin, MIKE SHE and MIKE FLOOD 1D & 2D), software for storm water modelling (MIKE Urban+ and PCSWMM) software for groundwater modelling and management (MIKE FEFLOW, Hydro-Geo-Analyst & Visual MODFLOW) and equipments for advanced terrestrial and aerial survey using Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV), DGPS and Total Station. The department also having the facilities of GeoWeb server platform and application to process large-scale geospatial data and handle, analyze and manage UAV and satellite imageries.

Sponsored Research and Industrial Consultancy Projects

  • Ultra-high resolution Aerial Survey and Design & Network Planning of UGPL Irrigation System in Bilaspur District, Chhattisgarh. (Funded by MB Construction, Ahmadabad Gujarat; Co-agency: Water Resources Department, Bilaspur, Govt. of CG) – Completed
  • Assessment of proposed Ahiran to Gajrinalah link project (project cost ~750Cr.) for water supply to Bilaspur smart city. (Funded by Water Resources Department, Bilaspur, Govt. of CG) – Completed
  • 3D UAV Mapping and Designing-Estimation of Narwa Restoration Plan in Patan Block, Durg. (Funded by MGNREGA, Zila Panchayat, Durg, Govt. of CG) – Completed
  • A Study on the Recent Floods in the Territories of Chhattisgarh Upstream of Hirakud Dam. (Funded by Water Resources Department, Govt. of CG) – Completed
  • Designing and Estimation of Group Water Supply Scheme Under Jal Jeevan Mission Using Aerial Survey in Dhamtari & Balod District. (Funded by Public Health Engineering Department, Govt. of CG) – Ongoing
  • Improving urban flood hazard vulnerability assessment with GDR using MCDM approach. (Collaborative Research Project (CRP) Funded by TEQIP-III) – Completed
  • Assessment of critical ground water potential zones of Kharun River basin using integrated approach of geospatial techniques. (Collaborative Research Project (CRP) Funded by TEQIP-III) – Completed

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Duration: 2 yrs. The Candidates should have passed Senior Secondary (10+2) or an equivalent Examination in five subjects taken together including compulsory subjects of Physics, Mathematics and one subject of Chemistry/ Biotechnology/ Biology/ Computer Science & English with 60% marks in aggregate (40% marks in the case of SC/ ST Category). Or Passed Diploma examination with at least 60% marks (40% in case of candidates belonging to reserved category), subject to vacancies in the First Year, in case the vacancies at lateral entry are exhausted.
