Welcome to Clubs @UTD CSVTU

Chhattisgarh Swami Vivekanand Technical University (CSVTU), established in 2005, is a premier institution in Chhattisgarh dedicated to advancing technical education and fostering innovation. The university not only emphasizes academic excellence but also encourages holistic development through a vibrant array of student clubs and organizations. These clubs provide a platform for students to explore their interests, enhance their skills, and engage in extracurricular activities that complement their academic journey.

Dr. Tarkesheshwari Verma
Head of Club and Cultural Program

CLUBS

Programmers' Paradise

At PROGRAMMERS' PARADISE, our vision is to cultivate a dynamic community where members not only excel in coding but also become innovators and leaders in the ever-evolving landscape of technology. We envision a future where every member harnesses their potential, leveraging our platform to push the boundaries of creativity and problem-solving.

Sports Club

The Sports Club promotes physical fitness and healthy competition through various sports events, tournaments, and fitness challenges. We encourage teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship, offering opportunities for members to excel and discover new sports. Our activities aim to enhance physical well-being, foster camaraderie, and develop athletic skills.

Audio Video Club

The Audio Video Club provides hands-on experience in audio recording, video editing, and filmmaking. We host workshops on cutting-edge technology and techniques, allowing members to create compelling content and enhance technical skills. Our activities include film screenings, production projects, and collaborations, fostering a creative and innovative environment for audiovisual enthusiasts.

Eco Club

The Eco Club promotes environmental awareness and sustainability through activities like tree planting, recycling drives, and clean-up campaigns. We educate members on eco-friendly practices and encourage actions towards a greener future. Our initiatives aim to instill a sense of environmental responsibility and inspire sustainable living habits.

Literary Club

The Literary Club is a heaven for book lovers and writers, offering book readings, workshops on writing, and poetry slams. We provide a supportive environment for sharing work, receiving feedback, and improving literary skills. Our events foster a love for literature, encourage creative expression, and help members develop their unique voices and styles.

Dharohar Club

The Dharohar Club celebrates cultural heritage through events, workshops, and exhibitions. We provide a platform for learning and engaging with traditional arts, crafts, and customs. Our activities promote the diverse cultural heritage of our community, fostering a sense of pride and appreciation for our rich cultural traditions.

Vedic Mathematics Club

The Vedic Mathematics Club promotes the ancient system of Vedic Mathematics through workshops and sessions. We teach efficient calculation techniques, helping members develop strong mathematical skills and a love for numbers. Our activities make mathematics enjoyable and accessible, enhancing problem-solving abilities and mental agility.

Yuva Tourism Club

The Yuva Tourism Club fosters a passion for travel and exploration. We organize trips, tours, and cultural exchanges, promoting understanding and appreciation of global cultures through responsible and sustainable tourism practices. Our activities aim to broaden horizons, create memorable experiences, and foster a sense of adventure and cultural appreciation.
